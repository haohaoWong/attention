
Chinese Treebank

There are 3,007 text files in this release, containing 71,369 sentences, 1,620,561 words,
2,589,848 characters (hanzi or foreign). The data is provided in UTF-8 encoding,
and the annotation has Penn Treebank-style labeled brackets.
Details of the annotation standard can be found in the segmentation,
POS-tagging and bracketing guidelines included in this release.
The data is provided in four different formats: raw text, word segmented,
POS-tagged and syntactically bracketed formats.
All files were automatically verified and manually checked.
